For [[Espresso]] drinks.

- Wand goes in at spout
- Angle slightly
- Dip in all the way
- Start
- Pull up til slurping sound
- Depending on drink, add 30-80% volume
- Then dip in all the way, stir
- LET CUP SIT for a second - Clean wand
- Knock in table
- Stirring motion
- Done!
